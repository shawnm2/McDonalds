Summary

Executive pay has a negligible impact on the overall picture.

Any significant increase in pay will have an equal impact on the price of food.

A $15/hr wage would raise the price's by 21.28%.

More to explore: Is fast food a perfect competion Market? Is McDonald's a price taker or a price maker? Could it survive at higher prices? How many jobs would be affected by a significant price increase?

In his classic Fast Food Nation (2001), Eric Schlosser describes the industry's manic pursuit of standardization. The machines that do the cooking are foolproof, virtually no skills are required. Jobs that have been de-skilled can be filled cheaply, writes Schlosser. The need to retain any individual worker is greatly reduced by the ease with which he or she can be replaced.
